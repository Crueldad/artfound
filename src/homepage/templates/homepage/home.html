About

  • Our Humble Beginnings

    We have developed a business idea that would generate exposure to new artists, allowing for success to be determined by public opinion. The comments will be stored in a database and then analyzed to determine which piece of artwork receives the most positive reviews. This idea was developed in a programming courses and was our end of the semester project.

  • A Prototype is Born

    We have developed a prototype for our business idea that will be used to determine its success in the art world. We would like to receive feedback from art critiques, editors and art advisors if any additions can be made to our business concept. The prototype can display three pieces of artwork at a time on the HTML and at the moment, no dataset is created linking the piece of artwork with its overall popular opinion and most common words.

  • Transition to Full Service

    Upon positive feedback, we would further develop our prototype into a business website and generate a dataset revealing the above information. In order to transition to full service, we would need to advertise our services so that enough individuals can make comments for a dataset to be established on the upload artwork. We hope to have this transition completed six months after confirmation of feedback.
